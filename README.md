**Starlight Tree** is a Web3D Christmas experiment that explores the magic of particle systems and perspective.

Built with **React**, **Three.js**, and **React Three Fiber**.

The application presents a 3D scene initially shaped like a Christmas tree.
- **Interact:** Click and drag to rotate the camera around the scene.

- **Discover:** Watch for Santa's sleigh crossing the moon and a hidden holiday message as you explore the full 360-degree view.
